Current Open Positions

In addition to the general open call, we are currently searching to fill the following roles:

 

Student Assistant (HiWi, 10h/week) Project Coordination Large Language Model Privacy

We are searching for a student assistant to support the project coordination of a project on large language model privacy. The responsibilities include

  • Maintaining the project website
  • Coordinating and attending monthly project meetings
  • Performing independent literature research on large language models and their privacy
  • Contributing to the project reports

Qualifications: We are looking for Bachelor or Master students in the area of computer science or related fields. The candidate should be well organized, self-driven, have good grades in their study program, and, in the best case, would bring some experience in project work. Since the main language of this project’s meetings and the written reports is German, candidates are required to hold a B2 or above level in German.

 

Student Assistant (HiWi, 19h/week) Privacy of Vision Foundation Models in the Medical Domain

We are searching for a Master’s student to support our research on private adaptations of vision models in the medical domain. The responsibilities include

  • Performing independent literature research on privacy and vision foundation models
  • Implementing research ideas and running experiments in pytorch
  • Contributing independent writing to the project reports
  • Attending the bi-weekly project meetings
  • Maintaining the project website

Qualifications: We are looking for a Master’s students in the area of computer science or related fields. The candidate should have a thorough expertise in Python programming (including pytorch), strong interest in research, abilities to perform independent work, and good grades from their Bachelor’s program and current Master’s courses.
